What to we do

JTechos turns outsourcing on its head through a new approach called ‘insourcing’. Instead of shipping work ‘out’ overseas to unnamed people, we focus on finding you a top overseas candidate to work for you directly in a ‘work from home’ model. Geared specifically towards growing start-ups or lean/mean corporate divisions, our approach is cost-effective, globally responsible, and creates a ‘win-win’ on both sides of the ocean.

Whether you’re just starting your business or you’re on Fortune’s Top 500 List, Innovative Outsourcing wants to come alongside and provide you with the business-critical staffing and services you need to succeed. From bookkeeping to payroll to administrative services, we do it all! And you reap the benefits of highly specialized, professional staffing services without the legal and fiscal responsibilities of full-time employees.
